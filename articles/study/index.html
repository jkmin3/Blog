Top 10 Best Spots to Study at UIUC

May 1, 2022

  • study
  • cafe
  • uiuc

As a student who has studied numerous hours and enjoys a variety of different types of study environments, I believe I have a good idea on what the best study spots are in the Champaign-Urbana area. I’ll be diving into my top 10 study spots with short, but descriptive points for each spot.

Main Library

  • This is my favorite spot to study right now.
  • UIUC Building
  • Interior
    • Stained window designs
    • Harry Potter hall/oldies vibes
    • Very large space
    • Lots of natural light seep through
    • Lamps have a nice light emanating from them
    • Beautiful
  • Volume Level
    • Very nice and quiet
    • You can talk normally but more on the quiet side because the place is so big.
    • 3/10
  • Usual Hours
    • 8:30 am – 10:00 pm

Aces

  • This used to be my favorite, now it is a close second.
  • UIUC Building
  • Interior
    • Very tall
    • Hollow Middle – Can see the whole library from any floor
    • Big windows – See beautiful views
  • Volume Level
    • A little louder than quiet
    • I once had a zoom meeting there, but I still spoke quietly
    • 5/10
  • Usual Hours
    • 8:30 am – 12:00 am

Siebel Center for Design

  • Very enjoyable place to study
  • UIUC Building
  • Interior
    • Modern
    • Geometrical
    • Technologically advanced vibe
  • Volume Level
    • Able to talk normally
    • Can be loud
    • 7/10
  • Usual Hours
    • 7 am – 12 am
    • People may have classes here

Campus Instructional Facility (CIF)

  • Nice classrooms to study in
  • UIUC Building
  • Interior
    • Modern
    • Has nice rolling chairs
    • Large windows
  • Volume Level
    • Depends on people there, but can talk normally
    • Had friends use the classrooms for karaoke
    • 7/10
  • Usual Hours
    • 7 am – 11 pm
    • Students attend class here

Sidney Lu (Mechanical Engineering Building)

  • Nice classrooms and outside tables to study at
  • UIUC Building
  • Interior
    • Modern
    • Has nap pods/stations
    • Has some couches
    • Futuristic look
    • Starbucks inside
  • Volume Level
    • Can’t be too loud
    • Can talk normally
    • Depends on people around you
    • 6/10
  • Usual Hours
    • Hours are not specified
    • Study there till you get kicked out
    • Mornings, students have classes

Champaign Public Library


  • Pretty library to study at
  • Off-Campus
  • Interior
    • Has many wonderful books
    • Large windows
    • Comfortable chairs
  • Volume Level
    • Quiet place to study
    • Can talk to your friends normally but not too loud
    • 4/10
  • Usual Hours
    • 9 am – 9 pm
    • Changes depending on the day

Cafeteria and Company

  • Very cute café
  • Off-Campus
  • Interior
    • Cute lights
    • Have fun board games to play
    • Just has a nice café vibe
  • Volume Level
    • Can have loud conversations
    • Can get loud
    • 9/10
  • Usual Hours
    • 8 am – 6 pm

The Literary

  • Small cozy bookstore
  • Off-Campus
  • Interior
    • Have few tables
    • There is a bar for coffee and alcoholic drinks
    • Sell books
  • Volume
    • Can be loud
    • 8/10
  • Usual Hours
    • 9 am – 9 pm

Harvest Market

  • Second floor of supermarket
  • Off-Campus
  • Interior
    • Has many tables
    • Free flavored water sometimes
    • Sell food in cafeteria
    • Buy drinks and snacks there
  • Volume
    • Store plays music
    • Can be loud
    • 8/10
  • Usual Hours
    • 6 am – 10 pm

Tenko Tea

  • Small Bubble Tea Store
  • Off-Campus
  • Interior
    • Has around five tables
    • Sells delicious bubble tea
    • Has board games
    • Cute place
  • Volume Level
    • Place plays music
    • People can be loud
    • Depends on amount of people there
    • Some nights are not good to study here
    • 5/10 – 9/10
  • Usual Hours
    • 12 pm – 12 am

Grainger

  • Honorable Mention
  • UIUC Building
  • Interior
    • A little musty of 1st and 4th floor
    • 2nd and 3rd floor don’t look too bad
    • Has an Espresso Royalle
  • Volume Level
    • 1st and 4th floor can be loud
    • 2nd and 3rd floor have to be very quiet
    • 1st and 4th floor: 8/10
    • 2nd and 3rd floor: 2/10
  • Usual Hours
    • 8:00 am - 3:00 am

Honorable Mentions: Union, Caffé Bene in Champaign and Urbana, Paradiso, Espresso Royale on Neil, Art Mart, and all other libraries.
